About me

Hello World! I am academically trained accountant metamorphosing into a software engineer. I have been working in tech for 8 years in various roles within software engineering. I started my career as a software tester consultant which helped me understand the end to end software development lifecycle,specifically the test life cycle,the intricacies of functional and GUI testing and solidify my interest in software engineering. For the past 7 years I have been working with the Department of Finance for the City of New York in functional, performance and datawarehouse testing and production support. To acheive this goal, I decided to complete my graduate program in software development from Boston University along with a graduate certificate in Web Application Development. Equipped with my degree and years of experience in the tech world, I hope to begin my journey as a Web developer focusing primarily on backend technologies and learning towards a full stack role.
